Program Overview

What is the TechMaster Program?

The AT-TIBYAN TechMaster Program is a 12-month intensive bootcamp designed to equip students with essential skills for a successful tech career. This program provides hands-on experience in designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software applications and systems. In addition to technical training, students will learn project management and business development to ensure a well-rounded education that prepares them for the job market.
